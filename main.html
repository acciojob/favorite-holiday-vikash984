Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city on the island of Honshu. It's famous for its beautiful temples, traditional wooden houses, imperial palaces, and stunning gardens. Known as the cultural heart of Japan, Kyoto offers a perfect blend of ancient history and modern beauty.

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine – Home to the iconic thousands of vermilion torii gates.
  2. Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) – A stunning temple covered in gold leaf.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove – Walk through the peaceful and mesmerizing bamboo forest.
  4. Gion District – A famous geisha district with traditional tea houses.
  5. Philosopher's Path – A scenic route lined with cherry blossoms in spring.
  • Kaiseki Ryori – A traditional multi-course meal that is both artful and seasonal.
  • Yudofu – A warm, comforting dish made of boiled tofu near temple areas.
  • Matcha Sweets – Enjoy rich green tea desserts like ice cream and mochi.
  • Saba-zushi – A type of sushi made with pickled mackerel.
  • Tsukemono – A variety of Japanese pickles served with most meals.
